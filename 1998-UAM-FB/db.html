Databases at EMBnet/CNB

At EMBnet/CNB we maintain a number of databases of various biological interest. All of them are updated daily so we can always offer the latest version to anybody that is interested in them.

The databases are maintained in our public FTP server so everybody can access them and copy anyone that may be of interest to their local computer. You do not need any special privileges to access them, just login as anonymous and get whatever you want. Subscribers have also access to many of them through specialized user interfaces that provide a powerful and easy to use environment that eases localizaton and analysis of their data.
