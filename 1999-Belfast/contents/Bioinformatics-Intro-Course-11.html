There's more than one way to do this!First, make a backup copy (e.g. cp myseq.seq myseq.backup ). If your sequence file is in a "standard" recognised format, e.g. fasta or staden : use a specialised GCG conversion program: fromfasta, fromstaden etc.Alternatively, you can use the readseq program available from the HGMP telnet or www menus.
