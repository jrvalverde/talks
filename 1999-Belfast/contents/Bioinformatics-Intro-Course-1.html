1 COURSE OBJECTIVE

To take computing novices through molecular biology computing (bioinformatics), with plenty of practicals. To provide people with an overview of what is out there, concepts and some common tasks. Practicals will try to follow typical mini projects so that the relevance is apparent.World wide web (www) version:http://www.hgmp.mrc.ac.uk/Courses/Intro_3day/index.htmlFurther depth will be given on the more advanced courses.
